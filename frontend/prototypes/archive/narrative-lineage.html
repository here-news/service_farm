A devastating fire at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po, Hong Kong, has resulted in multiple fatalities and the displacement of thousands of residents. The blaze, which swept through seven high-rise residential towers, has raised concerns about safety standards and negligence related to ongoing renovations.
• The fire at Wang Fuk Court displaced thousands of residents and resulted in at least four confirmed deaths.

• Authorities have arrested 14 individuals linked to the renovation project on suspicion of manslaughter and corruption.

• Residents had previously raised concerns about the flammability of materials used in the building's renovations.
